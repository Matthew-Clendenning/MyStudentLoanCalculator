My Student Loans Calculator

What is the purpose?

My Student Loan Calculator is a simple and easy to use tool, designed to help college students determine the amount of money they need to save per month in order to have smooth monthly payments for their loans after graduation.

How it works:

To begin using this calculator, you first need to enter the total amount of your loan. Known as your principal amount (e.g. 7500). Next, determine the fixed interest rate that is placed on your loan (e.g. 4.99). Additionally, determine what the length, or loan term, is on your loan. Federal student loan terms usually range from 10 to 30 years. Lastly, enter how long until you graduate (e.g. 4).


Disclaimer: Do not use dollar signs ($), or percent signs (%), when entering in your values.
